About Our

Project

Due to pandemic, the experience of studying in a classroom sharing camaraderie with your peers is no longer possible. A class like environment will motivate the students to attend classes more regularly so as to create a fun and interesting way of learning just by sitting at their houses with a nostalgic touch to the modern way of learning. The concept of MOOCs and the constraints of online education motivated us to design a solution which does not limit the the user’s exposure to online among the peers in his class but also enables them to interact, learn and get exposure from students from other countries.

Our problem statement is,

High school students lack the motivation to attend online classes because they find it monotonous and difficult to comprehend in practical courses due to the unavailability of proper equipment and minimal interaction with instructors.

After may discussions, interactiong with our target audience, and after understanding their needs, we come-up with the following solution statement -

Our solution explores virtual reality as a medium to give our user an experience by by bringing them in a virtual environment where they can work with equipments virtually, through various gestures while interacting with peers and students. The exposure the user is getting through this environment is not only confined to the user’s classmates but also involves foreign students without any language barriers in a common learning zone as a class.
